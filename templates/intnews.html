International News

International news covers events happening across the world, outside of one's own country. It includes news related to politics, conflicts, disasters, economic developments, and cultural events from different regions and countries. With globalization, international news has become increasingly important, as events in one country can have significant impacts on others, highlighting the interconnectedness of the world. Understanding international news is crucial for individuals and governments to make informed decisions and stay aware of global trends and developments.
